About HealHaven

HealHaven is an innovative platform designed to bridge the gap between education and healthcare. Our mission is to ensure students receive timely health support while creating a nurturing environment for learning and well-being.

Key Features:
  • Communication interface for reporting health issues directly to department doctors.
  • Automatic escalation to department teachers if no response is received within 5 minutes.
  • Dedicated profile pages for students and teachers.
  • Health chatbot offering quick assistance and guidance.
  • Login and registration features for secure access.
  • Blog platform for sharing posts and messages.
  • Search functionality: Doctors/teachers can search student profiles, while students can search blog posts.
  • Built with Django, HealHaven combines the power of technology and simplicity to deliver an impactful healthcare solution for educational institutions.

HealHaven is not just a platform, it’s a vision to make healthcare accessible, efficient, and integrated into educational institutions.
